World Perspectives

About This Website

The World Perspectives website concept is to see the world through someone else’s eyes. It is a profile based website that lets you wirelessly connect to a portable camera and ‘stream’ your life. The website can be connected to Facebook, but if you want your profile to be anonymous it’s possible, when you are all signed up you can request a viewing of other profiles, or watch people streaming their life. The request will be sent and if they accept, you will go to a confirmation about how long the viewing will be. The Camera is a back facing portable cam that can be clipped to your clothing. The idea of this is to show the world in different perspectives. The target audience would be people that are interested in different parts of the world without leaving your living room. By signing up to the World Perspectives website you will be able to access live streams, past videos and request viewing of live feeds. Once you have logged on you can upload videos made from your part of the world via a portable camera.
